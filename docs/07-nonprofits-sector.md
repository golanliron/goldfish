## Israeli Social Sector Map

### Scale
- **42,000 registered nonprofits** in Israel
- **~8,000 active** (significant operations)
- **70 billion NIS** total sector turnover
- **~15% of GDP** (including healthcare, education)

### Government Funding Channels
1. **Direct tenders** — competitive bidding
2. **Support regulations** (תקנות תמיכה) — criteria-based
3. **Service purchase** (רכש שירות) — contracted services
4. **Matching grants** — co-funding models

### Key Government Bodies for Nonprofits
- Registrar of Associations (רשם העמותות)
- Government Companies Authority
- Ministry of Justice — nonprofit regulation
- State Comptroller — nonprofit auditing

### Sector Trends (2026)
1. **Post-Oct 7 recovery** — massive shift to resilience, trauma, community rebuilding
2. **Mental health boom** — #1 funding priority across all funders
3. **Digital transformation** — nonprofits investing in tech/data
4. **Impact measurement** — funders demanding evidence-based outcomes
5. **Collaboration over competition** — funders prefer multi-org partnerships
6. **Periphery focus** — Negev and Galilee getting increased attention
7. **Youth agency** — young people as partners, not just beneficiaries

### Onboarding Questions (for new Goldfish users)
When a new org registers, Goldfish needs:
1. Organization name + registration number
2. Year founded
3. Annual budget
4. Staff count
5. Main domains (what you do)
6. Target populations (who you serve)
7. Geographic regions
8. Age groups served
9. Current funders
10. Biggest challenge in fundraising
