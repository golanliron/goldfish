## What is JFNA?

- **JFNA** (Jewish Federations of North America) = umbrella for ~150 federations + 300 communities
- Combined annual fundraising: **$2+ billion**
- Grant portals: **smapply** (SurveyMonkey Apply) — used by JUF, Pittsburgh, Palm Beach, JFNA
- After Oct 7: **$908M** emergency campaign, 4,200 grants to 877 orgs in 208 Israeli communities
- Federation Finder: https://www.jewishfederations.org/federation-finder
- JFNA Contact: Jessica.Mehlman@JewishFederations.org | info@JewishFederations.org | 212-284-6500

## How Goldfish Uses This Data

1. **Org-to-Federation matching** — by domain (education, youth, welfare, disabilities), population, geography
2. **Email outreach** — personalized pitch using grants contact + program name
3. **Timing** — sync submissions to federation grant cycles (most: Nov-Dec intake, June decisions)
4. **Women's Funds** — dedicated channel for women/girls programs
5. **Innovation Funds** — channel for new models (Hopa's Radar, AI tools, etc.)
6. **Israel programs** — 11+ federations with explicit Israel/amutot eligibility
7. **Security & Emergency** — post-Oct 7 funding streams still active at most federations
