## ENTRY STRATEGY FOR GOLDFISH USERS

1. **Direct Israel Grants** (accessible, $3K-$200K): Sarasota overseas grants, LA Israel Grants, Phoenix Strategic Initiative
2. **P2G Partnerships** (relationship-based): Match org geography to federation partner region
3. **Women's Funds** (separate track): Philly Women of Vision, Atlanta JWFA, Portland Women's Giving Circle
4. **Emergency/Rebuild** (current window): Position as long-term recovery partner, not emergency
5. **Foundation Grants** (larger, competitive): CJP Ally Challenge, JUF Breakthrough Fund

**Key trend:** All federations moving from emergency → "Rebuild Israel" — ideal timing for youth education orgs
